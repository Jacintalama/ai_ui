Selected Series

Long-form work

Series 01

Wilderness

Three years traversing remote mountain ranges across four continents. This series documents the raw, unfiltered beauty of places untouched by roads — glacial valleys, high-altitude meadows, and ridge lines at the edge of the inhabited world. Shot on medium format film, printed large.

View series

Series 02

Urban Rhythm

Cities are organisms. This series captures the pulse of metropolitan life — the geometry of concrete and glass, the anonymity of crowds, and the fleeting moments of stillness that open between the noise. Shot across Tokyo, New York, Lagos, and Berlin over two years.

View series

Series 03

Faces

A long-term portraiture project exploring identity, vulnerability, and presence. Each sitting is unhurried — often two to three hours of conversation before a single frame is made. The resulting images are candid in spirit even when staged in form.

About

<%= APP_NAME %>

Based between New York and Cape Town, I have spent the better part of fifteen years making images in places most people never reach. My practice sits at the intersection of documentary precision and a painterly sensitivity to light — work that aims to be honest about the world while still finding beauty in it.

I work on long-term personal projects alongside editorial and commercial commissions. My prints are held in private collections across Europe, North America, and South Africa. I am represented for editorial work by a select agency and accept a limited number of commercial assignments each year.
