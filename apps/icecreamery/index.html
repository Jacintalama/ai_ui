Our Story

🏡

The Icecreamery was born in the summer of 1998 when Maria and Luca Fontaine converted their grandmother's kitchen in the south of France into a tiny creamery. What started as a weekend hobby — perfecting custard bases and experimenting with lavender and salted caramel — quickly became the talk of the village. By the following summer, the queue stretched around the block.

Today, three generations later, we still churn every batch by hand using full-fat Jersey cream from Dubois Farm just 12 kilometres away. Our sugar comes unrefined from a small cooperative in Réunion, and we source every fruit at the peak of its season from local growers we've known for decades. We believe the best ingredients need no hiding — that's why our recipes have fewer than six components.

We now seat 40 guests in our sunlit parlour and welcome hundreds more through our take-away window each day. Whether you're stopping in for a quick cone or settling in for a leisurely sundae, our team is here to make the experience worth returning for. We are proud to be carbon-neutral certified since 2022 and donate 1% of each sale to local school nutrition programmes.
