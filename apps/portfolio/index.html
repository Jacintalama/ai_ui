01. About

Who I am

I'm a software engineer with a passion for building clean, useful tools that solve real problems. Whether it's a carefully designed API, a data pipeline that just works, or a UI that feels effortless to use, I care deeply about getting the details right.

Over the years I've worked across the full stack — from crafting PostgreSQL schemas and FastAPI services on the backend, to building responsive React frontends and automating workflows with tools like n8n and Docker. I enjoy the full arc of a product, from a blank editor to something live and reliable in production.

Outside of work I contribute to open-source projects, explore new languages (Go has been keeping me busy lately), and write about engineering trade-offs on my blog. I believe good software is mostly about clear thinking — and that the best code is the code that doesn't need explaining.

I'm currently open to interesting full-time or contract opportunities, especially in developer tooling, infrastructure, or product engineering.
