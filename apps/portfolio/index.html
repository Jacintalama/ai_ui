About me

I'm a software engineer specialising in AI platform engineering, with a focus on multi-tenant systems that let teams move fast without stepping on each other. I've built and operated production platforms that combine large-language-model orchestration, workflow automation, and real-time task management into cohesive, self-improving products.

I care deeply about end-to-end ownership - from infrastructure and data pipelines to the UI the user opens in the morning.
